About Time Capsule Builder

Preserve Your Memories for the Future

Welcome to Time Capsule Builder, the ultimate platform for capturing and preserving your most cherished moments. Whether it's a heartfelt letter to your future self, a collection of family photos, or a special video message, our platform allows you to create virtual time capsules that remain locked until a future date of your choosing.

What is Time Capsule Builder?

Time Capsule Builder is a web application designed to help users securely store digital memories and unlock them at a later date. Our goal is to add emotional value by enabling individuals to relive their past experiences, reconnect with loved ones, and pass down memories through time.

Key Features

  • Create Digital Time Capsules

    Store text, images, videos, and other media.

  • Scheduled Openings

    Set a future date for your time capsule to be unlocked.

  • Private & Secure

    Keep your memories safe with password-protected private capsules.

  • Easy Sharing

    Share unlocked capsules with family and friends.

  • Emotional & Meaningful

    Revisit precious memories or surprise a loved one in the future.

How It Works

  • Secure Storage

  • Unlock & Share
